A brief introduction to how I think and what I've been building.

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at the University of Engineering & Technology, Lahore, with a CGPA of 3.57 / 4.0. My work spans software development, generative AI tooling, and embedded systems — and the common thread is a commitment to building things that are both functional and maintainable.
